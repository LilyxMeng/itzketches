DEVICES/PROGRAMS/MATERIALS

WHAT DEVICE DO YOU USE TO DRAW?

I use an iPad Pro 11” for my digital artworks!

WHAT PROGRAMS DO YOU USE FOR YOUR DIGITAL ARTWORKS?

The main program I use is Procreate, however, I sometimes switch to clip studio paint (rarely) because they have better pixels, in my opinion.

WHAT BRUSHES DO YOU USE?

I mainly use the 6B pencil for sketching, the Gesinski Ink for inking, and the Flat Brush for shading, and also the Soft Brush.

DO YOU DO TRADITIONAL ARTWORK?

Not that often anymore, but I do still draw with a pencil now and then! It’s kinda a lot easier to draw via digitally since there is less of a mess and a lot easier.

DO YOU USE WATERCOLOUR? AND IF SO, WHAT BRANDS DO YOU USE?

Yes! I love watercolours! They were my favourite mediums aside from Copic markers.

I use multiple brands of watercolours:

  1. Schmincke Horadam Aquarell
  2. QoR
  3. Sennellier
  4. M. Graham
  5. Windsor and Newton

PRINTS INFORMATION

ARE THE PRINTS MADE OUT OF CARDSTOCK?

No, the prints I sell currently are not made from cardstock. They are on photo paper with a matte or glossy finish. However, I am working on getting new prints in the near future that will be cardstock! So stay tuned!
